About Me

Hey! I'm Amelia

I'm an aspiring graphic designer from Canberra, currently studying at Dickson College. My journey in design combines my passions for visual storytelling, music, and creative problem-solving.

I specialize in graphic design, digital illustration, photography, and media production. My work has been recognized with awards including 1st place in the CMET Light Rail Safety Week Competition (2025) and the Canberra Museum and Gallery Secondary School Art Prize (2023).

When I'm not designing, I'm immersed in music (contemporary and classical), theatre production, or exploring new creative computing techniques. I love bringing together different artistic techniques to create meaningful artworks.
