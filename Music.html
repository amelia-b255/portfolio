Music & Performance

Alongside my design work, I'm passionate about music and performance. I've been training in voice and musical theatre since 2018, and have performed in numerous productions, choirs, and competitions throughout Canberra and nationally.

  • ACT Primary & Senior Concert Choirs (2017-2024)
  • Musical Theatre training with Emma Griffiths and Dianna Nixon, Wild Voices
