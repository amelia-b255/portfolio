Light Rail Safety Campaign

A visual communication campaign promoting light rail safety awareness for Canberra Metro Operations, targeting young adults aged 15-24.

2025

The Brief

Project Overview

Canberra Metro Operations (CMET) commissioned me to develop a visual communication campaign promoting light rail safety awareness. Since the Canberra Light Rail's launch in 2019, public safety education has remained a priority for the operators.

My job was to create a visual campaign that educates and informs the target audience (15-24 years) about light rail safety. The designs needed to be both visually appealing and effective in communicating the safety message.

Requirements

  • All visual elements must be original work created using Adobe Creative Suite
  • Designs must be suitable for digital distribution and public display
  • All work must meet industry specifications for print and digital media
  • Target audience: 15-24 years old
  • Focus on light rail safety awareness and education
